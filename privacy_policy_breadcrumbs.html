Privacy Policy for BreadCrumbs

Effective Date: July 29, 2025

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at BreadCrumbs ("we", "us", or "our"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our social media and messaging app available on the Play Store.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register, we may collect your username, email address, and profile information.
  • Messages & Posts: Content you share, including posts and private chats, is stored securely and is only accessible by intended recipients.
  • Location Data: We collect location data to enable app functions such as localized feeds, finding nearby users, or enhancing user experience. Your location is used solely for these purposes and is not shared with third parties for advertising or analytics.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you interact with the app to improve performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To enable social features, messaging, and local content.
  • To enhance the safety and security of our users.
  • To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell or rent your personal or location data to third parties.
  • With Consent: We may share your information when you provide explicit consent.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

4. Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely and protected by industry-standard security measures.
  • Location data is only retained as long as necessary for functionality and is not stored longer than needed.

5. Your Rights

  • You can access, update, or delete your personal information at any time in the app’s settings.
  • You may revoke location permissions through your device settings, but some app features may not function as intended.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the effective date and notifying users within the app.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at: saiful@saifulratul.com

By using BreadCrumbs, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
